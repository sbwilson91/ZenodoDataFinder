### [VIOLIN: A modular framework for scalable reconciliation of heterogeneous interaction graphs](https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.07.21.604448)
**Luo, H.; Hansen, C. E.; Arazkhani, N.; Telmer, C. A.; Tang, D.; Zhou, G.; Spirtes, P.; Miskov-Zivanov, N.** | Systems Biology | 2026-03-28 | `large language model`

> Here is a 3-sentence summary of the abstract:

Researchers have developed a new framework called VIOLIN to integrate extracted molecular interactions from scientific literature with existing knowledge graphs. VIOLIN can accurately classify new interactions as confirmed, conflicting, or uncertain based on their similarity to established models, and its results are consistent across different datasets and models. The tool is publicly available and has been shown to be highly accurate in aligning with expert-curated data, making it a valuable resource for scientists working with molecular interactions.
